My works

Holding childhood - Lamp and Illustration

Project that intended to create two pieces, one 2D and one 3D that followed the same theme, in this case the childhood escape. A shooting star was created, a luminaire in a shape that resembles a small star and that is associated with a night light, along with a digital illustration of a character trying to reach the same star.

A moment far from problems - Pixelart animation

Project that invited the production of a 15 second video based on a complete song that should loop throughout the course of the song. In this project, the idea of escaping from problems was followed and then a minimally chaotic city was created with stresses always happening in the background while the protagonists of the video spend some time together ignoring what happens around them as much as they can.
